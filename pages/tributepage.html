"Doctor Strange"

Doctor Strange is played by actor Benedict Cumberbatch in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Doctor Strange is a fictional Marvel Comics character created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. The character first appeared in Strange Tales #110 (cover-dated July 1963). Doctor Strange serves as the Sorcerer Supreme, the primary protector of Earth against magical and mystical threats. Strange was created during the Silver Age of Comic Books to bring a different kind of character and themes of mysticism to Marvel Comics. For more information about the character, you can visit the Marvel Comics website.
